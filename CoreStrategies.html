Core Investment Process
Proprietary.
Repeatable. Proven.

The Runde Core process is based on a highly-diversified, fundamentally-based, process-driven approach to investing. The fundamental inputs are enterprise value, historic multiples of key metrics and forecasted values of key metrics. The process has been consistently applied for years and has proven replicable across market capitalizations and market environments. The process typically results in a portfolio of companies with higher price-to-book value ratios, higher forecasted earnings growth and a lower dividend yield.

The Runde Core process is based on a proprietary filter developed in-house. The filter allows for the screening of a large number of securities simultaneously in order to identify overvalued and undervalued equity securities.
