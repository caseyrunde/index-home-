Our Investment
Philosophy

A disciplined, process-driven approach to achieving solid risk-adjusted returns through highly diversified equity portfolios.

Overview
Built on process.
Driven by fundamentals.

Runde & Co. LLC seeks to achieve solid, risk-adjusted returns for its investors through the construction and management of highly diversified portfolios of equity securities. The investment strategy is a fundamentally-based, process-driven approach. Runde's objective methodology, based on a proprietary filter developed in-house, is designed to identify undervalued and overvalued stocks.

There are a number of benefits to a process-driven investment strategy and specifically to the Runde strategy. The primary benefit is that the Runde implementation creates and facilitates a dispassionate, unbiased approach to investing which is replicable and in which stock selection criteria can be uniformly applied. A secondary benefit is that a systematic approach allows for greater diversification of the assets which contributes to better risk-adjusted performance and a reduction in company-specific risks. Lastly, Runde portfolios have very low turnover which mitigates style-drift.

Core Principles
The foundation of our approach.

Fundamentals Drive Stock Price Returns

Our process is anchored in fundamental analysis — enterprise value, key metric multiples, and forecasted earnings — to identify securities the market has mispriced.

Disciplined Approach Is Replicable

A systematic, rules-based process removes emotion from investment decisions. Stock selection criteria are applied uniformly, creating a consistently replicable approach.

Diversification Reduces Corporate Event Risk

Greater diversification reduces company-specific risk and contributes to better risk-adjusted performance. Low portfolio turnover further mitigates style-drift over time.
