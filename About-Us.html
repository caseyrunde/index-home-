Elizabeth G. A. Runde
President & Co-Owner

Elizabeth is a graduate of Connecticut College and the New York University Graduate School of Business Administration.

She began her career in investment management in 1982 at Citibank in New York, where she managed fixed-income assets in excess of $2 billion for International Private Banking clients.

In 1986, she joined J.P. Morgan & Co., Inc., managing more than $250 million in multicurrency equity and debt accounts. She moved to Hong Kong in 1988 to establish and manage a J.P. Morgan investment management department.

She co-founded Runde & Co. LLC in 1992 with Casey Runde.

Casey Runde
Chief Investment Officer & Co-Owner

Casey is a graduate of the University of Wisconsin and the New York University Graduate School of Business Administration (with Distinction).

He began his career in 1978 as a portfolio manager with the Irving Trust Company in New York. Recruited by Citibank in 1981, he managed more than $1.5 billion in the Eurodollar and domestic bond markets, with work centered on credit analysis to uncover undervalued situations.

In 1985, he joined Prudential Securities specializing in Telecommunications and Technology. His combined experience in investment banking and investment management gives him a unique perspective on company valuations and the equity markets.

He co-founded Runde & Co. LLC in 1992 with Elizabeth Runde.
